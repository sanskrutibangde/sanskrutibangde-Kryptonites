Our Services

At TraceiT we are committed to supply Genuine & Reliable information taken from trustworthy sources. Our platform helps you to use various services at only a single platform. We provide services like COVID-19 detection by using chest x-ray, Mucormycosis (commonly known as Black Fungus) detection, Hospital beds availability, Oxygen availability and COVID-19 vaccine locator.

COVID DETECTION

Trace it is available to every user 24/7. Our system takes input a person's Chest X-ray and uses concept of Machine Learning & Neural network to determine if a person has COVID.

BED AVAILABILITY

TraceiT with other functionality also shows Hospital beds availability and Oxygen availability.

MUCORMYCOSIS CHECKER

TraceiT provides reliable self-assessment test which shows the possibility of having Black Fungus infection. It also provides details about different types of Mucormycosis infections.

VACCINE LOCATOR

Trace iT will be publishing up-to-date COVID-19 vaccine availability at hospitals. Will locate vaccines available near you.
